About BOSC Community Library

BOSC Community Library is an open-source digital library management system designed for educational institutions, public libraries, and knowledge-sharing organizations. Built with open-source principles at its core, it provides a transparent, scalable, and accessible solution for managing and distributing digital resources.

Our Mission

To democratize access to knowledge by providing a free, open-source alternative to proprietary library management systems, ensuring that educational institutions worldwide can share and access digital resources without vendor lock-in.
